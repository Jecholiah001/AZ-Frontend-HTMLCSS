Developing a Passion Project

A passion project is a personal project or endeavor that an individual undertakes driven by their personal interests and enthusiasm.
It is often a project that goes beyond one's regular work responsibilities and allows them to explore their creativity, learn new skills
or pursue a specific area of interest within the field of technology. The ways of developing a passion project are listed below.

  • Find passion project that matches your skillset.
  • Strategize how to achieve passion project.
  • Make your project visible.
